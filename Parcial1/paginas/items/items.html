ITEMS

Crops

Crops are plants that players can grow on their farm in Stardew Valley. They require watering and care to grow, and once harvested, they can be sold for profit or used in various recipes.

Animals

Animals are creatures that players can raise on their farm in Stardew Valley. They require feeding and care to produce various products such as milk, eggs, wool, and more. Animals can also be sold for profit or given as gifts to the villagers.

Milk

Milk is a product that can be obtained from cows and goats in Stardew Valley. Players can raise cows and goats on their farm and milk them using a milking machine. Milk can be used in cooking recipes, sold for profit, or given as gifts to the villagers.

Eggs

Eggs are a product that can be obtained from chickens and ducks in Stardew Valley. Players can raise chickens and ducks on their farm and collect eggs from their coops. Eggs can be used in cooking recipes, sold for profit, or given as gifts to the villagers.

Wool

Wool is a product that can be obtained from sheep in Stardew Valley. Players can raise sheep on their farm and shear them using a shearing machine to obtain wool. Wool can be used in crafting recipes, sold for profit, or given as gifts to the villagers.

Fish

Fish are aquatic creatures that players can catch in various bodies of water in Stardew Valley. Players can use a fishing rod to catch fish, which can be sold for profit, used in cooking recipes, or given as gifts to the villagers.

Foraged Items

Foraged items are items that can be found in the wild in Stardew Valley. They can be obtained by exploring the various areas around the town, such as the forest, mountains, and beach. Foraged items can include things like berries, mushrooms, flowers, and more. They can be sold for profit, used in cooking recipes, or given as gifts to the villagers.

Crafting Materials

Crafting materials are items that can be obtained from various sources in Stardew Valley, such as mining, foraging, or farming. They are used to create various tools, machines, and other items through crafting recipes. Crafting materials can also be sold for profit or given as gifts to the villagers.

Gifts

Gifts are items that can be given to the villagers in Stardew Valley to increase friendship levels. Each villager has specific preferences for gifts, and giving them items they like can help build stronger relationships. Gifts can include various items such as food, flowers, minerals, and more.
